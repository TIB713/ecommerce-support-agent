SECTION 1: Overview

A replacement sends a duplicate or like item to satisfy a valid claim—wrong item shipped, lost package confirmed by carrier, irreparable defect under warranty triage, or stock-preservation strategy when refund would strand seasonal inventory. Replacements are not offered as a courtesy for buyer's remorse on final-sale goods unless Executive approval documents a brand-risk exception.

This policy defines when replacement supersedes refund, expedite tiers, inventory substitution rules, and handling when the original item must be returned versus kept.

SECTION 2: Eligibility Rules

Wrong SKU in box: replacement ships same day or next business day when inventory available; customer returns mis-shipped item using prepaid label within fourteen (14) days. If return not received within twenty-one (21) days, charge placeholder MSRP per terms accepted at replacement request.

Carrier-lost, inventory confirmed shipped and not trackable delivered after trace: replacement or refund at customer choice for in-stock SKUs under $750; above $750 requires Fraud review before replacement. Customer must complete affidavit once per household per rolling twelve (12) months for lost claims exceeding $200 aggregate.

Defective within thirty (30) days of delivery with no repair path in five (5) business days: replacement authorized; after thirty (30) days, Warranty policy governs repair vs replace.

If cosmetic downgrade replacement is offered (open-box), disclose condition grade; customer may insist on new-in-box if stock exists and price differential under 10%.

SECTION 3: Exceptions

Limited editions: if sold out, offer waitlist conversion to refund plus $25 credit or substitute model with price adjustment—customer chooses.

Subscription shipments: replacement dose replaces only the affected cycle; do not double next cycle without Pharmacy or Regulatory sign-off where applicable.

Conflicting inventory: if warehouse shows phantom stock, cancel replacement order proactively, notify customer, pivot to refund with expedited shipping fee refund if they originally paid express. Partial shipment replacement: replace only missing lines; do not upsell bundles.

Marketplace: replacements initiated by seller; our platform may mask seller address—use RMA bridge workflow.

SECTION 4: Processing / Resolution Rules

Create child order linked to parent with zero-dollar offset authorization; shipping method matches original paid tier or better if error was ours. Log reason code WRONG-SKU, LOST, DEFECT-ADV, etc.

Return-first vs ship-first matrix: high fraud score requires return scan before ship except urgent medical device SKUs on physician attestation list.

Partial refund plus replacement prohibited except Finance-approved split resolutions for tax nuance. Document supervisor ID for any override.
