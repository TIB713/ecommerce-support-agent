NORTHLAKE COMMERCE — SHIPPING AND DELIVERY POLICY
Effective: 2026-01-01.

SECTION 1 — SHIPPING METHODS
1.1 Standard: Typically three (3) to seven (7) business days after fulfillment for domestic orders; not guaranteed unless you purchase a guaranteed delivery upgrade where offered.

1.2 Expedited: Two (2) business days after fulfillment in many metro areas; rural areas may take longer.

1.3 Overnight: One (1) business day after fulfillment where available; orders must pass cutoff times displayed at checkout.

SECTION 2 — PROCESSING TIME
2.1 Processing is one (1) to two (2) business days for in-stock items unless a product page states otherwise during sales events.

2.2 Presale and backorder items ship on the estimated date range listed at purchase; dates may shift due to supplier delays.

SECTION 3 — ADDRESS CHANGES
3.1 You may request an address change before label creation. After label creation, rerouting is not guaranteed and carrier fees may apply.

3.2 Address changes to a different country are generally not allowed; cancel and reorder if cancellation remains available.

SECTION 4 — SIGNATURE AND SAFE DROP
4.1 High-value shipments may require signature. If no one is available, the carrier may hold at a facility or reattempt per carrier rules.

4.2 “Authority to leave” options, where available, waive certain liability for theft after delivery; you accept this risk when selecting the option.

SECTION 5 — DELAYS AND FORCE MAJEURE
5.1 We are not liable for delays due to weather, natural disasters, pandemics, labor strikes, carrier capacity, customs holds, or other events outside our reasonable control.

5.2 If a delay exceeds fourteen (14) days beyond the estimated range, you may cancel unshipped items unless a legal exception applies.

SECTION 6 — SPLIT SHIPMENTS
6.1 Orders may ship in multiple packages. You are charged shipping according to the cart rules; per-item fees may apply for heavy items.

SECTION 7 — TRACKING
7.1 Tracking numbers appear in Order History when available. Tracking may not update on weekends for some carriers.

SECTION 8 — LOST PACKAGES
8.1 If tracking shows no movement for seven (7) business days domestic, open an investigation. Refunds or replacements may occur after carrier confirmation.

8.2 If tracking shows delivered but you did not receive, we follow the Missing Items workflow and may require a police report for high-value claims.

SECTION 9 — INTERNATIONAL OVERVIEW (DETAILS IN INTERNATIONAL POLICY)
9.1 Duties and taxes may be collected at checkout (DDP) or upon import (DDU) depending on service selected.

SECTION 10 — EDGE CASES
E1: Customer enters wrong ZIP; package returns; reshipment fees may apply.

E2: PO boxes: some carriers cannot deliver oversized items to PO boxes; order may cancel or require alternate address.

E3: Military APO/FPO: longer transit times; guaranteed delivery upgrades may not apply.

E4: Perishable goods: delayed delivery may void freshness guarantees; partial refunds may apply.

E5: Scheduled delivery missed due to customer unavailability may incur redelivery fees.

E6: Consolidation warehouses: additional handling days apply.

E7: If carrier strikes begin after purchase, we notify options: wait or cancel unshipped.

E8: Route deviations due to security screening are outside our control.

E9: If item is marked delivered to a locker, pickup must occur within locker time limits; expiration may cause return to sender.

E10: Freight curbside delivery does not include inside delivery unless purchased.

This policy is subject to carrier terms incorporated by reference.
