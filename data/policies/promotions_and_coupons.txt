NORTHLAKE COMMERCE — PROMOTIONS, COUPONS, AND PRICE MATCH RULES
Effective: 2026-01-01.

SECTION 1 — COUPON TYPES
1.1 Percent-off coupons apply to eligible items before tax, excluding shipping unless explicitly stated.

1.2 Fixed-amount coupons require a minimum subtotal where disclosed on the coupon.

1.3 Single-use coupons become void after successful checkout unless the promotion states multi-use.

SECTION 2 — STACKING
2.1 Unless a promotion explicitly allows stacking, only one promotional code may be used per order.

2.2 Automatic cart promotions may combine with free shipping thresholds if stated in the campaign terms.

SECTION 3 — EXCLUSIONS
3.1 Exclusions include gift cards, services, extended warranties, and certain brands that opt out. Excluded brands are listed in campaign fine print.

3.2 Marketplace items may be excluded from site-wide coupons at the seller’s discretion.

SECTION 4 — PRORATION ON RETURNS
4.1 If you return part of an order that used a coupon, the discount may be forfeited or reallocated, reducing your refund consistent with the Refund Policy.

4.2 Buy-two-get-one promotions require returning all promotional units to recover full refund amounts.

SECTION 5 — PRICE MATCH POLICY (WHERE OFFERED)
5.1 If a price match program is active, it applies to identical SKU, same model year, same color, sold by an approved competitor list posted on our site.

5.2 Price match requests must occur before purchase or within seven (7) days after purchase if the program allows post-purchase adjustments.

5.3 We exclude clearance, liquidation, membership club pricing requiring paid membership, bundle mis-matches, and pricing errors.

SECTION 6 — ABUSE AND FRAUD
6.1 Creating multiple accounts to reuse new-customer coupons violates policy; we may void discounts and close accounts.

6.2 Coupon resale or publication of single-use codes may lead to deactivation.

SECTION 7 — ADVERTISING MISTAKES
7.1 Obvious pricing errors may be corrected before shipment; you will be notified and may cancel if you disagree with the corrected price.

SECTION 8 — LOYALTY AND CREDITS
8.1 Loyalty points accrue per published earn rates; points cannot be purchased and have no cash value except where converted per program rules.

SECTION 9 — EDGE CASES
E1: Customer applies coupon then price match; the lower net price method applies as stated in campaign rules; not both if prohibited.

E2: Partial backorder: coupon validity may require shipment by a deadline; if missed, discount may be voided.

E3: Tax-inclusive jurisdictions: discount applies to pre-tax subtotal where tax law requires.

E4: Refunds involving coupons may take longer due to reconciliation with promo partners.

E5: Employee discount cannot combine with public coupons unless HR policy allows during specific events.

E6: If a coupon fails at checkout due to technical error but eligibility was valid, Customer Care may issue a one-time courtesy credit.

E7: Flash sales with limited inventory: rainchecks are not offered unless explicitly stated.

E8: Bundle discount requires all items; removing one item post-purchase voids bundle price on retained items per refund rules.

E9: Manufacturer rebates are separate; follow rebate instructions; our coupons do not stack with mail-in rebates where prohibited.

E10: Currency-specific coupons work only in the currency issued.

Contact Promotions Support for disputes with documentation.
