NORTHLAKE COMMERCE — RETURNS AND EXCHANGES POLICY
Effective: 2026-01-01. Applies to consumer purchases of physical goods sold by Northlake Commerce.

SECTION 1 — RETURNS VERSUS EXCHANGES
1.1 A return ends in a refund or store credit depending on your selection and eligibility. An exchange replaces your item with another variant (size/color) or a similar SKU subject to inventory availability.

1.2 Exchanges are not guaranteed: If inventory is unavailable, we may offer a refund or backorder at your discretion.

SECTION 2 — INITIATING A RETURN OR EXCHANGE
2.1 Start in Order History within the return window for your category. You will receive a return merchandise authorization (RMA) and a prepaid label for many domestic orders. Some categories require customer-paid return shipping.

2.2 Include all original items, accessories, and packaging. Incomplete returns may be rejected or partially credited.

2.3 Multiple items: You may combine items from the same order in one shipment if the RMA allows. Do not mix unrelated orders without authorization.

SECTION 3 — TIMELINES
3.1 Domestic returns must be scanned by the carrier within fourteen (14) days of RMA issuance unless extended by Customer Care.

3.2 After we receive the item, inspection typically takes three (3) to five (5) business days. High-volume seasons may extend processing.

SECTION 4 — CONDITION STANDARDS
4.1 “Like new” means no scratches beyond light handling, fully functional, factory reset for devices where applicable, and activation locks removed.

4.2 Apparel must have tags attached for full refund where tags are required on the product page.

4.3 Open-box items purchased as open-box must be returned with the same open-box accessories listed on the invoice.

SECTION 5 — EXCHANGE LIMITATIONS
5.1 Price differences: If the new item costs more, you pay the difference. If it costs less, the difference may be refunded as store credit or to the original payment method depending on workflow availability.

5.2 Exchanges across categories (e.g., shoes to headphones) are generally not allowed unless the original item is out of stock and Customer Care approves a substitute.

SECTION 6 — RESTOCKING AND LABEL FEES
6.1 If you use a prepaid label but the return is ineligible (e.g., outside policy), we may deduct label costs from any refund.

6.2 Oversized items may incur additional return shipping charges disclosed during RMA creation.

SECTION 7 — THIRD-PARTY SELLER ITEMS
7.1 Items fulfilled by marketplace sellers follow the seller’s return policy displayed at checkout. Northlake may facilitate messaging but does not set seller-specific windows unless the seller opts into our standard policy.

SECTION 8 — EXCEPTIONS
8.1 Hazardous materials returns require special carrier handling; do not use standard labels unless instructed.

8.2 If you return an item that is not the item purchased (wrong product in box), we will investigate potential fraud and may escalate.

SECTION 9 — EDGE CASES
E1: Customer returns a phone with a cracked screen claiming it arrived damaged; if inbound QA shows impact damage inconsistent with shipping damage patterns, we may deny or partially refund.

E2: Customer buys two sizes intending to return one; allowed if both remain in returnable condition within the window.

E3: Bundle return where one item is missing reduces refund per bundle repricing rules in the Refund Policy.

E4: If carrier loses the return shipment, customer must provide drop-off proof to open a carrier claim; refunds may be delayed pending claim outcome.

E5: Exchanges for limited drops may be waitlisted; if the drop sells out, refund may be the only option.

E6: International returns may require customs documentation; customer is responsible for accurate declarations; misdeclaration may lead to abandonment fees.

E7: If an RMA expires, a new RMA may be denied if outside the return window unless an exception applies.

E8: Store pickup returns can be processed in-store where stores exist; online-only SKUs must mail return.

E9: If a return is received after a refund was already issued due to error, we will attempt recovery per Refund Policy.

E10: Gift returns without receipt may receive store credit at lowest promotional price in the prior ninety (90) days.

This policy complements the Refund Policy and Cancellation Policy. In case of conflict on timing, the stricter window for the customer’s benefit applies where required by law.
