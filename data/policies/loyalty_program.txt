NORTHLAKE COMMERCE — LOYALTY PROGRAM TERMS (NORTHLAKE PLUS)
Effective: 2026-01-01.

SECTION 1 — ENROLLMENT
1.1 Enrollment is free where offered; accounts must be in good standing.

1.2 Employees may be ineligible for certain promotions.

SECTION 2 — EARNING POINTS
2.1 Points earn on eligible net purchases excluding tax, shipping, and gift card purchases.

2.2 Bonus events may have category caps.

SECTION 3 — REDEMPTION
3.1 Points redeem at published rates; rates may change with thirty (30) days notice.

3.2 Points expire on inactivity after twenty-four (24) months unless extended by promotion.

SECTION 4 — TIER BENEFITS
4.1 Tiers (Silver/Gold/Platinum) unlock perks like extended return windows where stated.

4.2 Tier status is evaluated quarterly based on rolling spend.

SECTION 5 — EXCLUSIONS
5.1 Marketplace purchases may earn reduced or zero points per seller settings.

SECTION 6 — FRAUD
6.1 Point farming via fake returns may lead to forfeiture.

SECTION 7 — TERMINATION
7.1 We may end the program with reasonable notice and redemption period.

SECTION 8 — EDGE CASES
E1: Returns claw back points proportionally.

E2: Partial refunds adjust points.

E3: Price match refunds adjust points.

E4: Employee purchase points delayed.

E5: Business accounts separate loyalty unless linked.

E6: Gift recipient earns points if purchase tied to member account.

E7: Subscription renewals earn points if eligible SKU.

E8: Charitable donation SKUs may be excluded.

E9: Compliance holds freeze redemption temporarily.

E10: Merger of accounts requires identity verification.

Escalate tier mismatch complaints to Loyalty Ops.
