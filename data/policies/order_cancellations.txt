NORTHLAKE COMMERCE — ORDER CANCELLATIONS POLICY
Effective: 2026-01-01.

SECTION 1 — WHEN YOU MAY CANCEL
1.1 Before shipment: Most orders can be canceled in Order History if status is “Processing” and no shipment label has been created. Cancellation is not guaranteed if fulfillment is automated and extremely fast.

1.2 After label creation: Cancellation may be blocked. You may refuse delivery or use the Returns Policy after delivery.

1.3 Backorders: You may cancel a backordered line item until it ships unless the product page states the deposit is non-refundable for special manufacturing runs.

SECTION 2 — PAYMENT CAPTURE TIMING
2.1 Authorizations may appear at order time; capture may occur at shipment. If you cancel before capture, the authorization typically falls off per bank timelines.

2.2 If capture already occurred, refunds follow Refund Policy timelines after cancellation confirmation.

SECTION 3 — BUNDLED PROMOTIONS
3.1 Canceling one item in a bundle may void bundle pricing. The remaining items may be repriced to standalone prices before refund calculation.

3.2 Buy-one-get-one promotions may require returning all promotional units to cancel the deal fairly.

SECTION 4 — SUBSCRIPTIONS AND ADD-ONS
4.1 Subscription first-box promotions may require completing a minimum term if disclosed at signup. Early cancellation may incur a fee equal to the discount received.

4.2 Installation services scheduled with third parties may require twenty-four (24) hours notice to cancel without fee; same-day cancellation may be charged per partner terms shown at booking.

SECTION 5 — CUSTOM AND MADE-TO-ORDER
5.1 Custom items cannot be canceled after production starts. Production start is defined as when materials are allocated and work orders are issued, which may be within hours.

5.2 If we fail to ship within the promised build window without notice, you may cancel for a full refund unless you accept a revised date in writing.

SECTION 6 — PREORDERS
6.1 Preorders can be canceled until the charge date if no non-refundable deposit applies.

6.2 If a deposit is non-refundable, cancellation forfeits the deposit as disclosed.

SECTION 7 — SELLER-FULFILLED ORDERS
7.1 Marketplace sellers may have different cancellation windows. Our platform will show seller-specific rules.

SECTION 8 — FRAUD AND ABUSE
8.1 Rapid cancel/repurchase patterns to exploit promotions may lead to order holds and account review.

SECTION 9 — EDGE CASES
E1: Customer cancels while package is mid-flight; interception is not always possible; refund may process after return.

E2: Partial shipments: canceling unshipped portion is allowed; shipped portion follows returns.

E3: Gift orders: purchaser can cancel before shipment; recipient cannot cancel purchaser’s payment method orders without authorization.

E4: Corporate purchase orders: cancellation may require written approval from the buyer’s AP department.

E5: If cancellation fails due to system error but customer was charged, we escalate and refund with priority.

E6: Weather holds: if carrier suspends service, cancellation may reopen briefly; communicated via email.

E7: If item becomes illegal to ship to your region, we cancel and refund without penalty.

E8: Waitlist conversions: canceling a waitlist conversion may remove your priority status for future drops.

E9: Mixed cart with digital items: digital delivery may block full-order cancellation; partial cancellation rules apply.

E10: If a duplicate order is detected, we may cancel duplicates automatically and notify you.

Contact Customer Care for edge cases not covered here.
