SECTION 1: Overview

This policy governs tender acceptance, authorization and capture timing, failed payment recovery, gift card and split-tender sequencing, currency presentation, and chargeback coordination. Meridian Market processes payments through PCI-DSS compliant gateways; we do not store full PAN on merchant servers. Displayed prices default to USD for meridianmarket.com US storefront; international shoppers may see localized currency estimates but settlement occurs in USD unless the regional site is the native multi-currency instance.

Security objectives: reduce fraudulent authorizations, comply with PSD2 SCA where applicable, and minimize customer friction for low-risk baskets.

SECTION 2: Eligibility Rules

Acceptable methods: major credit and debit brands, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay, branded stored-value gift cards, and approved enterprise procurement cards for B2B accounts. We do not accept personal checks, wire for consumer carts under $2,000, or cryptocurrency on the standard storefront.

Authorization: card authorizations hold funds at order submit for seven (7) days on standard SKUs; custom build holds may extend to fourteen (14) days with notification. Capture occurs at ship or digital fulfillment for most goods; digital instant delivery may capture at order commit. Partial capture matches partial ship in split shipments.

Split tender applies gift cards first, then promotional credits, then external payment for remainder. Refunds reverse in proportional tiers unless a line maps entirely to one instrument.

SECTION 3: Exceptions

Insufficient funds or issuer decline: one automated retry after twenty-four (24) hours for subscription continuity orders; others require customer to update payment. AVS/CVV mismatch on high-AOV orders may cancel automatically—customer may reorder after bank confirms billing address.

Chargebacks: upon receipt of dispute, Finance freezes related refunds and may offset future credits. Customers claiming "product not received" while tracking shows delivery require Fraud review before merchant acceptance of liability.

Currency rounding: two-decimal presentation; FX fees from issuing bank are between customer and issuer. Conflicting promo: "pay with Brand X card get 5% off" cannot combine with another card-specific issuer promo—lowest net for customer wins after system validation.

Regulated installments (BNPL): governed by partner Lender of Record; merchant handles only order fulfillment and return triggers to BNPL ledger.

SECTION 4: Processing / Resolution Rules

Agents never request full card numbers or CVV; use secure pay-by-link for manual recharges. For duplicate charge visible on statement, verify double order IDs; if single order double-captured, apply expedited refund with Finance ticket within one (1) business day.

B2B NET30 accounts: past-due suspension at thirty (30) days; reactivation requires treasury clearance—not handled in consumer payment queues.

Payment anomaly logging: suspected BIN farming or velocity attacks—block session, preserve headers for Fraud. Partial refund on financed order must route through BNPL adjustment API to avoid customer still owing full plan.
