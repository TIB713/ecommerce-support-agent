NORTHLAKE COMMERCE — PRIVACY, DATA USE, AND COMMUNICATIONS PREFERENCES
Effective: 2026-01-01.

SECTION 1 — DATA COLLECTION
1.1 We collect order data, device data, and support interactions to fulfill orders and improve services.

1.2 Payment details are processed by PCI-compliant processors; we do not store full card numbers.

SECTION 2 — USE CASES
2.1 Fraud prevention, delivery coordination, warranty support, and legal compliance.

SECTION 3 — SHARING
3.1 We share data with carriers, payment processors, and service providers under contracts.

3.2 We may share with law enforcement when legally required.

SECTION 4 — RIGHTS
4.1 Depending on region, you may access, correct, delete, or port data subject to exceptions.

SECTION 5 — MARKETING
5.1 Opt out of marketing emails via unsubscribe links; transactional emails may continue.

SECTION 6 — RETENTION
6.1 Order records retained for seven (7) years for tax and fraud purposes unless law requires longer.

SECTION 7 — CHILDREN
7.1 Our general storefront is not directed to children under sixteen (16); parental consent required where applicable.

SECTION 8 — EDGE CASES
E1: Data subject requests for deleted orders may be denied if legal hold applies.

E2: Corporate buyer requests employee data removal: must prove authority.

E3: Lawful intercept requests handled by legal team only.

E4: Analytics opt-out via cookie banner where required.

E5: Phone support recordings retained ninety (90) days unless escalated case extends.

E6: Account merge requires strong verification.

E7: Breach notifications per jurisdictional timelines.

E8: Gift recipient data minimization: purchaser’s email used for receipts unless recipient account created.

E9: Seller KYC data handled per marketplace seller agreement.

E10: Research datasets anonymized; opt-out where required.

Escalate privacy legal requests to DPO inbox.
