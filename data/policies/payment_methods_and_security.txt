NORTHLAKE COMMERCE — PAYMENT METHODS, AUTHENTICATION, AND SECURITY
Effective: 2026-01-01.

SECTION 1 — ACCEPTED PAYMENT TYPES
1.1 Major credit and debit cards, digital wallets where enabled, installment plans through approved partners, and store credit.

1.2 Bank transfers may be available for B2B accounts only.

SECTION 2 — AUTHENTICATION AND FRAUD CHECKS
2.1 We may require CVV match, AVS match, 3-D Secure, or OTP verification for high-risk transactions.

2.2 If authentication fails, the order may be canceled to protect account holders.

SECTION 3 — INSTALLMENTS
3.1 Installment offers are subject to partner approval. If approved, you enter a contract with the lender; we are not the lender.

3.2 Early payoff rules follow lender terms.

SECTION 4 — GIFT CARDS AND SPLIT PAYMENT
4.1 You may combine gift cards with other payment methods up to platform limits.

4.2 If an order is partially paid by gift card and partially refunded, refunds may prioritize card reversal rules per network guidelines.

SECTION 5 — CURRENCY AND CONVERSION
5.1 You are charged in the currency selected at checkout. FX fees may be applied by your bank for international cards.

SECTION 6 — FAILED PAYMENTS AND RETRIES
6.1 If capture fails at shipment, we notify you to update payment within forty-eight (48) hours or the order may cancel.

SECTION 7 — CHARGEBACKS
7.1 Chargebacks may result in account restrictions until resolved.

SECTION 8 — ACCOUNT SECURITY
8.1 Enable two-factor authentication. We may force password resets on suspicious logins.

SECTION 9 — EDGE CASES
E1: Card expired between order and shipment: update required or cancel.

E2: Corporate card requires PO number: enter at checkout; missing PO may delay B2B fulfillment.

E3: Prepaid cards without billing address may fail AVS repeatedly.

E4: Partial authorization holds for multi-shipment orders may confuse banks; we provide invoice breakdown on request.

E5: Wallet tokenization errors: try direct card entry.

E6: Recurring subscription payment fails: grace period three (3) days then pause.

E7: Fraud alert from bank declines charge: customer must call bank then retry.

E8: Cryptocurrency is not accepted unless a pilot program explicitly enables it for specific SKUs.

E9: If customer disputes duplicate charge but two orders exist, provide both order numbers.

E10: Refund to canceled card: bank handles via account routing rules; may become check.

Escalate payment anomalies to Payments Ops.
