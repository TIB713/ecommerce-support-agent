NORTHLAKE COMMERCE — SUBSCRIPTIONS, SOFTWARE LICENSES, AND DIGITAL DELIVERY
Effective: 2026-01-01.

SECTION 1 — DIGITAL DELIVERY
1.1 Digital products are deemed delivered when the download link is made available or the license key is displayed.

1.2 Refunds for digital goods are generally not available after delivery except where required by law or explicit written exceptions.

SECTION 2 — SUBSCRIPTION TERMS
2.1 Subscriptions renew automatically at the stated interval unless canceled before the renewal date.

2.2 You may cancel future renewals in Account Settings; canceling does not refund the current period unless a promotional guarantee applies.

SECTION 3 — FREE TRIALS
3.1 Trials convert to paid unless canceled during the trial window. Reminder emails are courtesy, not guaranteed.

SECTION 4 — PRICE CHANGES
4.1 We may change subscription prices with thirty (30) days notice; you may cancel before the new price takes effect.

SECTION 5 — BUNDLED HARDWARE
5.1 Hardware promotions tied to subscription commitments may charge a recovery fee if you cancel early, as disclosed at purchase.

SECTION 6 — LICENSE RESTRICTIONS
6.1 Software licenses are non-transferable unless the EULA allows.

SECTION 7 — CLOUD SERVICES UPTIME
7.1 Uptime targets are aspirational unless a separate SLA is purchased for business accounts.

SECTION 8 — EDGE CASES
E1: Customer disputes digital delivery but IP logs show access: denial of refund.

E2: Family sharing violations may revoke licenses.

E3: Student verification fails mid-subscription: may revert to standard pricing.

E4: App store purchases follow platform rules; we cannot override Apple/Google refund decisions.

E5: Preorder software key delays: cancel before key issuance allowed.

E6: Annual plan proration on early cancel: typically no cash refund; credit may be offered case-by-case.

E7: Gifted subscription: recipient manages cancel; purchaser cannot always revoke.

E8: Enterprise keys mistakenly sold retail: recalled and replaced.

E9: DRM server shutdown legacy titles: compensation may be store credit per announcement.

E10: Beta access subscriptions: may end when product launches.
