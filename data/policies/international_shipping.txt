NORTHLAKE COMMERCE — INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING, DUTIES, AND IMPORT COMPLIANCE
Effective: 2026-01-01.

SECTION 1 — AVAILABILITY
1.1 Not all items ship to all countries due to vendor restrictions, export controls, or carrier limitations.

1.2 Some electronics require export licenses; we block checkout if restricted.

SECTION 2 — DUTIES AND TAXES
2.1 Delivered Duty Paid (DDP): Estimated duties and taxes collected at checkout for supported lanes.

2.2 Delivered Duty Unpaid (DDU): You pay duties upon import; delays may occur at customs.

SECTION 3 — PROHIBITED ITEMS
3.1 Lithium battery restrictions vary by mode and country; some SKUs cannot ship air internationally.

3.2 Certain agricultural and food items are prohibited.

SECTION 4 — RETURNS FROM ABROAD
4.1 International returns may require customs declarations; mislabeling may forfeit refunds.

4.2 Return shipping costs may be customer responsibility unless the error was ours.

SECTION 5 — ADDRESS FORMATS
5.1 Provide Roman characters where possible; failed transliteration may delay delivery.

SECTION 6 — TIMELINES
6.1 International transit may take two (2) to four (4) weeks depending on lane and customs.

SECTION 7 — DOCUMENTATION
7.1 Invoices are provided electronically; commercial invoices accompany the package for customs.

SECTION 8 — EDGE CASES
E1: Customer refuses to pay duties on DDU: package may return; refund minus shipping.

E2: Customs destroys prohibited item: no refund if customer ordered prohibited SKU against warnings.

E3: Brexit/region changes: VAT rules may shift; checkout displays current rule.

E4: Remote islands: surcharges apply.

E5: Military diplomatic pouches: special rules; contact support.

E6: Temporary import for trade shows: not supported for consumer checkout.

E7: Split customs valuation disputes: escalate to trade compliance.

E8: Currency refunds in international orders return in original currency where possible.

E9: Lost inbound return international: carrier claim timelines vary.

E10: Sanctions: if country becomes sanctioned mid-order, we cancel unshipped.

Escalate export control questions to Compliance.
